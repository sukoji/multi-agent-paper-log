## 한 줄 요약

LLM을 policy·comm·reward로 쓰는 hybrid MARL 시도 정리.

## 배경 · 문제 정의

Pure RL sample inefficient. LLM prior + RL fine-tune 또는 LLM as teammate simulator가 트렌드다.

## 핵심 방법

- LLM proposes high-level macro actions, low-level RL
- Multi-agent debate as implicit policy ensemble
- Textual reward from LLM critic
- Sim environments: overcooked, SMAC text wrapper

## 실험 · 결과

- Sample efficiency gain in some hybrid setups
- Text reward noisy
- No dominant paradigm yet

## 한계 · 비판적으로 본 점

Mostly research prototypes. Eval not standardized.
