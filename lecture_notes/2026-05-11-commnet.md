## 한 줄 요약

CommNet: continuous communication channel을 differentiable하게 학습.

## 배경 · 문제 정의

Agent간 learned communication protocol이 coordination을 enables한다. CommNet은 broadcast summed message를 쓴다.

## 핵심 방법

- Encode obs → hidden state
- Communication: average hidden states across agents
- Decode to action
- End-to-end backprop through comm channel

## 실험 · 결과

- Cooperative tasks beat no-comm baseline
- Emergent discrete-like protocols in continuous channel
- Influenced later TarMAC, IC3Net

## 한계 · 비판적으로 본 점

Toy sims only. LLM natural language comm is not differentiable.
